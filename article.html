Echelon marches on: Continuing our mission of building the startup ecosystem in Southeast Asia

The Echelon roadmap has been updated, read on to find out more

As 2016 comes to an end, we can’t help but look back at this amazing year. From raising our Series A to our hosting our first Echelon in Vietnam, 2016 has been a blast.

While we’re still reeling from the all the glitter of 2016, why not take a sneak peek at what we’re offering for 2017?

Our new (and updated) roadmap for 2017 is now out! From fan favourites like Echelon Indonesia to Echelons in new countries, we’re looking to kick off 2017 with a bang. Our mission to build Southeast Asia’s brand is looking brighter than ever, as we complete the Southeast Asian journey:

  • March: Penang, Malaysia
  • March: Bangalore, India
  • May: Bangkok, Thailand
  • June: Singapore
  • July: Almaty, Kazakhstan
  • August: Jakarta, Indonesia
  • September: Vietnam
  • November: Philippines

Hint: I smell lahpet in the distance. A new frontier to be revealed, maybe?

A new Echelon experience for everyone

We believe the entrepreneur should no longer be constrained by time, distance or geography. In 2017, those who participate will be treated with a new Echelon experience, witnessing the evolution of our 2 day tech and business conference into a digital platform taking place all year round.

Interested? Stay tuned to the latest news, or get in touch to get involved.
