ANCHOR SOUTHBOUND SUMMIT 2018

ANCHOR 2018 is community-led initiative with a vision to promote diverse economic opportunities and collaboration between SEA and Taiwan, in line with the Taiwan's Government's New Southbound Policy. In its inaugural year, ANCHOR sets forth to support SEA's startups in growing and scaling in East Asia using Taiwan's hardware and IoT strengths as a base, and driving Taiwan's startups and SMEs in expanding globally. ANCHOR's flagship regional startup search also will bring 15 of SEA's top tier startups to Taiwan in March to put their best pitch forward, build valuable connections and discover the potential of Taiwan's startup ecosystem. Applications are now open!
