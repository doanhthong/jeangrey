What do TOP100 startups enjoy?

TOP100 prides itself as the most diverse startup search, with a stringent curation process to match.

Each year, investors, corporates and key ecosystem leaders look to Echelon Asia Summit and TOP100 as the go-to platform and conference for business partnerships, quality deal flow, and comprehensive market access oppotunities. All participating startups receive a comprehensive suite of benefits, and the qualified final 100 startups enjoy the full prize stack, including:

Insight

Access insights from the experts and community builders about Southeast Asia and Taiwan's tech ecosystem

Connection

Tap on opportunities to forge new strategic partnerships across markets, verticals and ecosystems

Funding

Expand your networks with a one-stop platform for funding and investments across Southeast Asia and Taiwan

Market Access

Connect with local tech corporates and MNC in Taiwan for solid BD opportunities, direct market access and potential M&A.

City Qualifiers

Gather over great conversation and drinks, with shortlisted startups invited to pitch live to a panel of judges and investors. Meet and mingle with community, industry and technical leaders to level up your business. Get your game face on!

Alumni who've made it

With opportunities for participating startup to meet over 5,000 key ecosystem players, 300+ investors, getting involved in TOP100 and Echelon means countless qualified partnership opportunities to grow their business, get funded, build their team, and get recognised as the ones to watch all across the region.

How to Apply

Are you
  • A pre-Series A, early stage startup?
  • Based in or interested in entering Asia Pacific?
  • Comfortable with pitching in English?
  • Able to commit to the TOP100 programme through to June 2018’s Echelon Asia Summit in Singapore?
  • Seeking Funds or Business opportunities with the region’s most active investors and tech corporates?
  • Keen to showcase your product or service to the region’s thought leaders and first mover tech adopters?
  • Looking for extended exposure across tech and business media publications and broadcast stations?
If it's a resounding yes to the above - go forth: Apply below!
